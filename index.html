About

User empathy
isn't a skill.
It's a superpower.

Most product managers learn about users from research reports and data dashboards. I learned by sitting next to them at 35,000 feet — resolving problems in real time, under pressure, with no room for error.

After years as a flight attendant with IndiGo and Qatar Airways, and a stint leading content strategy at MoreTasks, I discovered that what I was doing all along was product thinking: identifying pain points, finding elegant solutions, and making complex things feel effortless.

Now I'm formalising that instinct — pursuing Product Management with Generative & Agentic AI at BITSoM (BITS Pilani), building systems, writing PRDs, and learning to ship products that solve real problems.

"I don't just want to build products. I want to build the right ones — shaped by genuine understanding of the people who use them."
